Course intro

Goals

  • Example functional genomics analysis
  • Understand what bioinformatics entails
  • Start building skill set in relevant tools

Format

  • Five weeks, each with a different focus
  • Each week will carry the analysis into the next stage
  • Two main teachers per week, but most of us aim to be on Teams as much as possible
  • All announcements in Cambro
  • Other communication in Teams

My week

  • 09:00 - 12:00 Interactive Lectures
  • 13:00 - 16:00 Exercises (self-study)

Grading

  • One assessment per week (max 5 short questions). Graded pass/fail and all must be passed.
  • One final presentation, two groups at a time. Graded VG/G/U. Each teacher watching as well as the other group will grade the presentation and the grade will be a consensus of the two grades.
  • Grading criteria: Technical accuracy, presentation visuals, oral presentation
